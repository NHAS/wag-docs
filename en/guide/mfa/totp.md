# Time-based One-Time Password

This method is by far the simplest MFA method to enable within wag.  

<div style="text-align:center">   
<img src="/images/mfa/totp.png"  class="shadow">
</div>

As long as you have an `Issuer` set, and enable "Time Based Code", your users will be able to register with the TOTP method. 
It is recommended to use Google Authenticator for this.
